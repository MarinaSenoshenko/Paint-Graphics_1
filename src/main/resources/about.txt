Paint application which contains 3 types of actions:
1) draw line (using Bresenham algorithm) with changeable width
2) draw n-corner figures, and stars with changeable count of corners, radius, turn
3) fill your picture (using span-fill algorithm)

When you modify the size of the panel that is being shown,
you are also changing the size of the image that will be produced
